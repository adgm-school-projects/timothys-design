Colombian La Vereda

This coffee has an intense fragrance and flavor combining exotic floral notes with balanced body and acidity. La Vereda's distinctive, fruity, and citric overtones can be tasted in every cup. A portion of the sales of La Vereda are donated back to the native coffee growing community in Caldas to support education for both coffee growers and their children.

View details »

Ethiopian Kimssa

From the lands where coffee was first discovered, Ethiopia is the grandparent of all coffees. The burgundy notes distinctive to Yirgacheffe provides a deliciously rich taste. A portion of sales is donated to the Kimssa school project supporting skills training for young people in this coffee growing community.

View details »

Italian Blend

One of our lighter dark roasts. A smooth blend characterized by a symbiotic balance of sweetness, smokiness and body. There is something for everyone in this cup. It spices up a romantic Italian afternoon accompanied with biscotti, rich cannoli, or gelato.

View details »

Las Nubes

Embedded in the smooth body of Las Nubes are sweet, fruity, and citrus notes. While the everyday coffee drinker will enjoy this delightful cup, its subtle complexity will also be apparent to coffee connoisseurs. A portion of sales is donated to York University's Fisher Fund for Neotropical Conservation for environmental study in the region of the Las Nubes Biological Preserve.

View details »

Nicaraguan Fair Trade Organic

This Fair Trade coffee is as pleasing for its mild nutty character, light acidity, and undertones of chocolate notes, as it is for its organic cultivation and Fair Trade certification - guaranteeing a reasonable economic return to the coffee growers and proper care of the local environment.

View details »

Women In Coffee

A blend of Costa Rican Tarrazu coffees, all from farms run by women. Rich in body and brightness, this coffee will shine at any time of the day or night. Fruit and chocolate in the aroma and cherry, lime, and melon notes in the cup. Finishing this coffee is the lingering sweet chocolate and light lemon notes. Partial proceeds from this blend will help support coffee-growing communities through improvements in the towns where these women live and work.
